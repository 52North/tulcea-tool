Citizen Science in Tulcea

We all love food. We all need clean water and energy. With this application you can find information on food, water and energy in Tulcea and by uploading information yourself you can help reseachers to understand how these fields are connected. More concrete, you can find and upload information about

  • local food producers in and around Tulcea,
  • production services like bakeries,
  • consumer preferences regarding e.g. the origin of food products,
  • water suppliers,
  • energy usage of citizens.

For more detailed explanation on how to use the application, please check the different topics presented in the navigation at the top of the page.
The application was developed in cooperation with the Tulcea City. We are currently testing the first version of the application. Share with us your opinion about it by clicking on the feedback link at the bottom of this page!
