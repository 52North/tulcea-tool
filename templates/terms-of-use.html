General conditions of use

Creating Interfaces - Tulcea is an open platform for citizen science, which allows citizens to access information about food, water and energy in Tulcea and by uploading personal information related to those mentioned, to help researchers understand how these fields are connected. More specifically, you can find and upload information about:

  • local food producers in and around Tulcea
  • production services such as bakeries
  • consumer preferences regarding e.g. origin of food,
  • water suppliers,
  • energy consumption of citizens.

PERSONAL INFORMATION

When information is requested through this site, it aims to identify the actors within the three elements studied: Food, Water. Energy. Consumer information is collected to identify in the city of Tulcea the general profile of the consumer for a better understanding of needs and a more personalized presentation to local producers and authorities. The study also tries to unite all the actors at administrative level responsible for the three elements studied.

The nature of the requested information refers to the identification data of the entities included in the three elements studied in Tulcea or with possible connections to them (name, address, telephone numbers, e-mail address), but may include other information, for example information on energy consumption aimed at improving knowledge about the element Energy.

In order to better meet the needs and questions of users of the site, the information requested through this site will be subject to electronic storage and processing.
